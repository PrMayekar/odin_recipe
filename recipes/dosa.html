Dosa

Description

Dosa is a thin, crispy South Indian crepe made from a fermented batter of rice and lentils. It is commonly served with coconut chutney, sambar, and a spiced potato filling.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice (preferably idli rice)
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black lentils)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • 2 boiled potatoes, mashed (for filling)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • Curry leaves

Recipe:

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind them into a smooth batter and let it ferment overnight.
  3. Add salt to the fermented batter and mix well.
  4. Heat a tawa (flat pan), pour a ladle of batter, and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin crepe.
  5. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges and cook until the dosa is golden and crisp.
  6. For the filling, heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies.
  7. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  8. Add mashed potatoes, turmeric, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Place the filling inside the dosa and fold it in half.
  10. Serve the dosa hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
