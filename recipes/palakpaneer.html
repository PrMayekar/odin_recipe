PalakPaneer

Description

Palak paneer is a popular North Indian vegetarian dish made with soft paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and pureed spinach (palak) cooked in a blend of aromatic spices.

Ingredients:

  • 250g spinach (palak)
  • 200g paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tbsp cream (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Recipe:

  1. Blanch the spinach: Boil water in a pot, add spinach leaves, and cook for 2-3 minutes. Drain and immediately transfer to ice-cold water to retain the green color.
  2. Blend the blanched spinach into a smooth puree and set aside.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them crackle.
  4. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  5. Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chili, and cook for another minute.
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil separates from the mixture.
  7. Stir in the turmeric, coriander powder, and garam masala, and cook for a minute.
  8. Add the spinach puree to the pan and mix well. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  9. Add the paneer cubes to the spinach gravy and simmer for 5 minutes.
  10. If using cream, stir it in just before turning off the heat.
  11. Serve hot, garnished with cream or butter, alongside naan, roti, or rice.
