Pav Bhaji

Description

Pav Bhaji is a famous Indian street food from Mumbai, consisting of a spicy mashed vegetable curry served with soft, buttered bread rolls called pav. It is a flavorful and filling dish that is popular all over India.

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup cauliflower, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 pav (bread rolls)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Recipe:

  1. Heat oil and butter in a large pan. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.
  2. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  4. Add tomatoes, green bell pepper, and cook until soft.
  5. Add the mashed potatoes, cauliflower, green peas, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Stir in the pav bhaji masala and red chili powder.
  7. Use a masher to mash the mixture while cooking, until everything is well combined.
  8. Add water to adjust the consistency and cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  9. Toast the pav with butter on a tawa (flat pan) until golden brown.
  10. Serve the hot bhaji with buttered pav, garnished with onions, lemon wedges, and coriander.
